Taking orders online is easy.
(ok, not that easy) but getting the order into the hands of the restaurant - now that's hard.

That's where eatabit.io comes in.

So, how does it work?

What if...you could hand orders directly to the restaurant?

In the world of online food ordering, the last mile is the longest mile. Fax machines, unreliable internet connections and long hold times all stand between you and the kitchen. Our Cloud to Ground Printing API gives your orders a direct, dedicated path right into the hands of the restaurant staff.

What if...you could track the status of your customer’s orders?

When people order food, they want to know two things: "How long will it be?" and "Did the restaurant actually get my order?" The eatabit.io API allows you to confidently answer these questions for your customers.

What if...you could eliminate the need for a call center?

You should not be phoning in your customers' orders. Period. Instead, leverage our API to print orders directly inside the restaurant. Only eatabit.io has this capability.
