Partner with us. We've got a guy on the inside...

Taking orders online is easy (ok, not that easy) but getting the order into the hands of the restaurant - now that's hard. That's where we come in. Partner with us, and your order gets priority delivery into the restaurant. Partnership is simple: you provide orders, we deliver them. Then we split the fee 50/50.

Talk to a standard API. REST is the best.

We provide a standard JSON API which allows you to view restaurants' realtime pickup and delivery times, create new print jobs and view status on existing jobs. So with one POST, your order is fromatted, transmitted and printed - done.

Print inside the restaurant. Paper is king.

Nothing beats a printed ticket. Our purpose-built, patented, 2-way printers are placed inside the restaurant - where all the action is. The printers alert the staff when orders are ready to print and supply you with realtime confirmations and fulfillment times.
