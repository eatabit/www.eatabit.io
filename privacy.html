Introduction

At Eatabit we are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal data. Any personal data that you share with us will be kept safe and secure. We want to be clear about how and when we collect data from you and how and why we use it. Our Privacy Notice explains in detail the types of data we may collect about you and how we will use and store your personal data.

About Eatabit

Eatabit is Pocket Restaurant, LLC, providing cloud printing services for restaurants and more. We are located in Charleston, SC.

What Personal Data Do We Collect?

When you contact us, we may collect some or all of the following:
  • First & last name
  • Company name
  • Job title
  • Email
  • Phone number
Furthermore, if you use the Eatabit printer management app, we also collect for analytics purposes:
  • IP address
  • Cell phone model (iPhone 11, Galaxy S9+, etc.)
  • Cell phone name (Jerry's iPhone, etc.)
  • Operating system version

Why Do We Need Your Personal Data and How We Use It?

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Investigate software bugs

When and How Do We Collect Your Data

We collect your information when you submit an email or contact form or use the printer management mobile app.

How Long Do we Keep Your Data

We keep your information for 12 months after your initial enquiry. Unless you become a client, at which time we retain your information for 7 years after you cease to be a client.

Sharing Your Data

We do not share or swap your data with any other organisation. There may be a need to disclose your personal data upon request to regulatory and government bodies as well as law enforcement agencies.
